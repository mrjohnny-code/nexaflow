Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really pay upfront?

No, our program is designed to eliminate initial costs. Qualified homeowners can switch to solar with no down payment, allowing you to start saving on energy bills from day one.

How do the government rebates work?

Our program helps you navigate and access available federal and provincial rebates. These incentives reduce the overall cost of your solar system, making it even more affordable. Our team will guide you through the application process.

How long does installation take?

Typically, solar panel installation can take anywhere from 1 to 3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your system. The entire process, from initial consultation to activation, usually spans a few weeks.

What provinces are eligible?

Our program is available to qualified homeowners in multiple provinces across Canada. Please check our eligibility form or contact us to confirm if your specific location is covered.
